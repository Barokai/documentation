We have updated our Node.js buildpack:
- it now supports Node.js versions `18.18.0`, `20.6.0`, `20.6.1` and `20.7.0`
- it now supports yarn `3.6.2`, `3.6.3`, `4.0-0-rc.49`, `4.0.0-rc.50` and 
  `4.0.0-rc.51`
